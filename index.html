ABOUT THE DISASTER

This book goes throught what happend on the night of April 26, 1986

Anatoly Dyatlov was deputy chief-engineer on his night shift in control of the Chernobyl reactor 4 plant. He was instucted to do a test on reactor #4 so it could pass the soviet's RBMK (reaktor bolshoy moshchnosty kanalny) reactor standards. While they were running the test the reactor exploded.
